About Us

Umlilo is an end to end service designed to enable Africans in the diaspora to seamlessly purchase, finance and install solar systems in their home country from anywhere in the world!

With the click of a button you can send energy to Africa and your loved ones back home.

Invest in Africa, Be the Spark that lights up the African continent!

Our Service

How It Works

Select a solar system
Step 1

Based on your requirements select a solar system that would be ideal for what you would like. If you are unsure, the Umlilo team is always on hand to assist

Submit a finance application
Step 2

Once you have chosen your desired solar system, you will be required to complete and submit a finance application

Financial Approval
Step 3

The Umlilo Team will review your application and will approve financing if all is in order

Delivery & Installation
Step 4

Your solar system will now be ready for delivery and installation to you specified address

Sleep Easy
Step 5

You will now be able to rest easy and enjoy your clean and renewable energy system
